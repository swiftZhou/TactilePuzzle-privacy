Privacy Policy

App: Tactile Puzzle
Effective date: June 10, 2026
Last updated: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tactile Puzzle ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our iOS application. The App is an offline tactile jigsaw puzzle experience with Story Mode and Free Mode. We respect your privacy and designed the App to collect as little information as possible.

1. Summary

  • No account registration or login is required.
  • We do not operate our own user database or backend servers for gameplay data.
  • Game progress and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.
  • In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not directly collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or mailing address
  • Precise geolocation or GPS data
  • Contacts, photos, microphone recordings, or camera images from your device
  • Health, financial, or government identification information
  • Browsing history outside the App

3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide core functionality, the App may store the following information locally on your device using Apple’s standard storage mechanisms (such as UserDefaults and on-device app storage):

  • Story Mode and Free Mode puzzle progress and completion status
  • Fragment positions and playthrough state for in-progress puzzles
  • App settings, including sound and haptic feedback preferences
  • Whether the full-content in-app purchase has been unlocked on this device
  • Your most recent Free Mode session configuration, where applicable

This information is used only to save your progress, restore your experience between app launches, and unlock purchased content. We do not transmit this locally stored gameplay data to our own servers.

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time non-consumable in-app purchase (The App offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock all content.) that unlocks additional story chapters and Free Mode content.

All purchases and payment processing are handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card number, billing address, or other payment credentials. Apple may provide us with purchase-related information necessary to verify entitlement and unlock content in the App. Apple’s handling of payment information is governed by Apple’s own privacy policy.

You may restore previous purchases using the "Restore Purchases" option shown in the App’s purchase screen.

5. Permissions and Device Features

The App does not request access to sensitive device permissions such as the camera, microphone, photo library, contacts, or location services. Optional sound and haptic feedback use standard iOS system features and do not involve uploading personal data.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party advertising networks, analytics trackers, social media SDKs, or cloud gameplay backends controlled by us. Apple system services involved in app distribution, updates, and in-app purchases are provided by Apple Inc.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the App does not require account creation and does not collect personal identifiers, parents and guardians can allow children to use the App under their supervision. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to address the request.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Locally stored App data remains on your device until you delete the App or reset the App’s local data through device settings where available. Uninstalling the App generally removes locally stored progress, settings, and unlock state stored on that device. Purchase entitlements tied to your Apple ID may still be restored through Apple after reinstalling the App.

9. Security

Because gameplay data is stored locally on your device, its security also depends on the security of your device and Apple ID. We recommend keeping your device software up to date and using device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID where available.

10. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding personal information. In practice, for this App you can:

  • Turn sound and haptic feedback on or off in Settings
  • Delete locally stored App data by removing the App from your device
  • Manage or revoke in-app purchase permissions through your Apple ID and App Store settings
  • Contact us with questions or requests related to this Privacy Policy

11. International Users

The App is available in multiple languages and may be downloaded in various countries and regions. If you use the App outside your home country, your information may be processed according to the laws of the jurisdiction where you use the device, as well as Apple’s policies as the platform provider.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App, legal requirements, or business practices. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after the updated policy becomes effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the App’s privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@zhouhai.app

隐私政策

应用名称：Tactile Puzzle
生效日期：2026 年 6 月 10 日
最近更新：2026 年 6 月 10 日

本隐私政策说明您在使用 Tactile Puzzle（以下简称「本应用」或「我们」）时，我们如何处理相关信息。 本应用是一款离线触觉拼图应用，包含故事模式与自由模式。 我们重视您的隐私，并尽可能减少信息收集。

1. 概要

  • 无需注册账号或登录。
  • 我们不运营独立的用户数据库，也不将游戏数据上传至自有服务器。
  • 游戏进度与偏好设置保存在您的设备本地。
  • 不使用第三方广告或统计分析 SDK。
  • 内购由 Apple 完整处理。

2. 我们不收集的信息

我们不会直接收集以下类型的个人信息：

  • 姓名、电子邮箱、电话号码或通讯地址
  • 精确地理位置或 GPS 信息
  • 通讯录、照片、麦克风录音或相机拍摄内容
  • 健康、金融或政府身份证件信息
  • 本应用以外的浏览记录

3. 设备本地存储的信息

为实现核心功能，本应用可能通过 Apple 提供的本地存储机制（如 UserDefaults 及设备本地应用存储）在您的设备上保存以下信息：

  • 故事模式与自由模式的拼图进度及完成状态
  • 进行中拼图的碎块位置与游玩状态
  • 应用设置，包括音效与触觉反馈偏好
  • 本设备是否已解锁完整内容内购
  • 最近一次自由模式会话配置（如适用）

上述信息仅用于保存进度、在再次打开应用时恢复体验，以及解锁已购买内容。 我们不会将此类本地游戏数据上传至自有服务器。

4. 应用内购买

本应用提供一次性非消耗型内购（一次性解锁全部内容）， 用于解锁额外故事章节与自由模式内容。

所有购买与支付流程均由 Apple 通过 App Store 处理。 我们不会收到或存储您的银行卡号、账单地址或其他支付凭证。 Apple 可能向我们提供验证购买资格及解锁内容所必需的相关信息。 Apple 对支付信息的处理受其自身隐私政策约束。

您可通过应用内购买页面中的「恢复购买」功能，恢复此前已完成的购买。

5. 权限与设备功能

本应用不会请求相机、麦克风、相册、通讯录或定位等敏感权限。 可选的音效与触觉反馈仅使用 iOS 系统标准能力，不涉及上传个人数据。

6. 第三方服务

本应用不集成第三方广告网络、统计分析工具、社交媒体 SDK，也不使用由我们控制的云端游戏后端。 与应用分发、更新及内购相关的 Apple 系统服务由 Apple Inc. 提供。

7. 儿童隐私

本应用面向普通用户。 我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。 由于本应用无需注册账号且不收集可识别个人身份的信息，家长或监护人可在其监督下允许儿童使用本应用。 如您认为儿童向我们提供了个人信息，请联系我们，我们将采取适当措施处理相关请求。

8. 数据保留与删除

本地存储的应用数据将保留在您的设备上，直至您删除本应用，或在设备设置中清除应用本地数据（如可用）。 卸载本应用通常会删除该设备上本地保存的进度、设置与解锁状态。 与 Apple ID 关联的购买权益，在重新安装应用后仍可能通过 Apple 恢复。

9. 信息安全

由于游戏数据保存在您的设备本地，其安全性也与您的设备及 Apple ID 安全相关。 建议您保持系统更新，并启用设备密码、Face ID 或 Touch ID（如可用）。

10. 您的权利与选择

根据您所在地区的适用法律，您可能享有与个人信息相关的权利。就本应用而言，您可以：

  • 在设置中开启或关闭音效与触觉反馈
  • 通过删除本应用来清除本地存储的数据
  • 通过 Apple ID 与 App Store 设置管理或撤销内购相关权限
  • 就本隐私政策相关问题联系我们

11. 境外用户

本应用支持多种语言，并可能在不同国家或地区提供下载。 若您在境外使用本应用，相关信息处理可能受您使用设备所在司法辖区法律，以及平台提供方 Apple 相关政策的约束。

12. 隐私政策变更

我们可能不时更新本隐私政策，以反映应用功能、法律要求或业务实践的变化。 更新后，我们将修订本页顶部的「最近更新」日期。 政策生效后您继续使用本应用，即视为接受更新后的政策。
